me too.

Tarana Burke founded the me too movement in 2006 to help survivors of sexual violence, specifically young women from lower economic backgrounds to heal. Then, in 2017, because of a hashtag started by Alyssa Milano, a viral conversation about this violence was forced to the forefront of mass media. What started as local, grassroots organizing, has expanded to a community larger than anyone could have imagined. The months since #MeToo spread on Twitter have been about radical community healing. Join the movement, help reclaim the narrative and help hold perpetrators accountable for their actions. You are not alone. Get educated. Learn how you can get involved. Say the words, call them on theirs, and finally remove this normalized behavior from our discourse. 1
